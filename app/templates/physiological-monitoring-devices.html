Physiological Monitoring Devices

Basic Life Monitoring Unit

MR compatible life monitoring of rodents providing

  • Display and analysis of physiological (ECG and respiratory) signals

  • Body temperature supervision and measurement

  • ECG or respiratory synchronized MR signal detection

  • Gating IN/OUT Triggers

Trigger Channel

Real-time input and output channels for the user control of external devices or triggered data acquisition by external sources, e.g. respiration or ECG

  • Four output lines

  • Two input lines

Body Temperature Conditioning System

Water based heating unit to condition the body temperature of (anesthetized) rodents via warming blankets or integrated water hoses in animal beds. The basic unit consists of:

  • Water reservoir with pump and thermostat

  • Hoses to connect to the warming blankets or beds
